# Privacy Policy — Aura Meter

_Last updated: 2026-07-04_

Aura Meter ("the App") is a novelty entertainment app. This policy explains what
information is handled when you use it. **The App is for entertainment only; aura
readings are randomly generated and are not a real measurement of anything.**

## Information we store on your device
The App stores your progress **locally on your device** using standard app
storage (`shared_preferences`). This includes your aura scan history, aura
points, streaks, achievements, unlocked frames, and settings. This data never
leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App or use
**Settings → Reset Progress**.

We do **not** require an account, and we do **not** collect your name, email,
contacts, photos, or precise location.

## Advertising
The App displays ads through **Google AdMob**. To serve ads, Google may collect
and process certain information such as your device's advertising identifier,
approximate (non-precise) location derived from your IP address, and
app-interaction/diagnostic data. This is governed by Google's policies:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from partner sites/apps:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

On iOS, the App may ask for permission to track (App Tracking Transparency). If
you decline, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant.

## Children
The App is not directed to children under the age required by your local law
(e.g. 13 in the US / 16 in parts of the EU). We do not knowingly collect
personal data from children.

## Your choices
- Reset all locally stored data via **Settings → Reset Progress**.
- Reset or limit ad personalization in your device settings (advertising ID).
- Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.

## Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
by updating the "Last updated" date above.
