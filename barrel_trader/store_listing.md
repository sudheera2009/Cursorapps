## Full description
Ever wanted to trade the energy markets without risking a cent? **BARREL** drops
you onto a fast, realistic oil & gas trading desk with a virtual $100,000 to
put to work. 🛢️📈

⚠️ Simulation only — BARREL does not use real money and does not connect to any
exchange. Prices are generated on-device for practice and entertainment.

✨ WHAT YOU GET
• Trade crude oil (WTI & Brent) and natural gas (Henry Hub & Dutch TTF)
• Plus refined products: heating oil and RBOB gasoline
• A live tape — prices tick every second on realistic charts
• Go LONG or SHORT and profit whichever way the market moves

📊 TRADE LIKE A PRO
• Clean buy/sell ticket with lot sizing and buying-power checks
• Mark-to-market equity, unrealized & realized P&L
• Average entry lines drawn right on the price chart
• Session open, day high/low and range meter

💼 TRACK YOUR EDGE
• Portfolio with per-position P&L and one-tap close
• Full trade blotter and win-rate stats
• Watchlist your favourite contracts
• Your account persists between sessions — pick up where you left off

Whether you're learning how futures markets move or just love the thrill of a
well-timed trade, BARREL is your risk-free energy trading playground.
