# Privacy Policy — BARREL

_Last updated: 2026-07-17_

BARREL ("the App") is a paper-trading market simulator. This policy explains what
information is handled when you use it. **The App is a simulation for education
and entertainment only; prices are randomly generated on-device, and the App does
not use real money or connect to any exchange or brokerage.**

## Information we store on your device
The App stores your account **locally on your device** using standard app
storage (`shared_preferences`). This includes your virtual cash balance, open
positions, trade history, watchlist, and settings. This data never leaves your
device and is deleted when you uninstall the App or use **Account → Reset
account**.

We do **not** require an account, and we do **not** collect your name, email,
contacts, photos, payment details, or precise location.

## No financial services
BARREL is not a broker, dealer, exchange, or financial advisor. It does not
execute real trades, hold funds, or provide investment advice. Nothing in the
App is a recommendation to buy or sell any security or commodity.

## Analytics & advertising
This version of the App does **not** include third-party analytics or
advertising SDKs and does not transmit usage data off your device. If this
changes in a future release, this policy will be updated accordingly.

## Children
The App is not directed to children under the age required by your local law
(e.g. 13 in the US / 16 in parts of the EU). We do not knowingly collect
personal data from children.

## Your choices
- Reset all locally stored data via **Account → Reset account**.
- Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.

## Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
by updating the "Last updated" date above.
