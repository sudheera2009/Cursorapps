## Full description

**Image Toolbox is the one app for everything you do with images — and it works
100% offline.** No sign-up, no uploads, no waiting. Your photos never leave your
phone.

**🗜️ Compress**
Shrink photos to save storage or hit an exact target size (e.g. "under 300 KB").
Perfect for email, uploads and forms.

**📐 Resize**
Scale by percentage, exact dimensions, or "max longest side" with one-tap
presets for web, social and thumbnails.

**🔄 Convert**
Switch between JPEG, PNG and WebP instantly. WebP can dramatically cut file size.

**✂️ Crop & Rotate**
Crop to any ratio, straighten, rotate and flip with a smooth editor.

**🎨 Filters & Adjust**
Beautiful presets plus fine control over brightness, contrast, saturation and
sharpness.

**💧 Watermark**
Protect your work with text watermarks — choose position, opacity and tiling.

**⚡ Recipes & Batch**
Save your favourite settings as one-tap recipes, and process dozens of images at
once. Export everything as a ZIP.

**🔒 Private by design**
Every operation runs on your device. We never upload your images.

Download Image Toolbox and take control of your photos.
