# Privacy Policy — Image Toolbox

_Last updated: 2026_

Image Toolbox ("the app") is designed to be **private by default**. This policy
explains what data the app does and does not handle.

## Your images never leave your device

All image processing — compression, resizing, format conversion, cropping,
rotating, filtering and watermarking — happens **entirely on your device**. The
app does **not** upload, transmit, or store your photos on any server.

## Photo and camera access

The app requests access to your photo library and camera **only** so you can
select or capture images to process, and to save results back to your gallery.
These permissions are used locally and nothing is shared.

## Data we store locally

The app stores small preferences on your device (default format/quality,
your saved recipes, and a lifetime "space saved" counter). This data stays on
your device and can be cleared from within the app or by uninstalling it.

## Advertising

The app may display ads via **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect device
identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads, in accordance with
Google's own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

On iOS, the app asks for App Tracking Transparency permission before any
tracking-based advertising identifiers are used. You can decline.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
personal information from them.
